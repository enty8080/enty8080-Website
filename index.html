About Me

I am an information security student and penetration testing software developer. I began my journey into programming and cybersecurity in 2019 at the Moscow School of Programming and currently pursuing my studies at South East Technological University (SETU) in Carlow, Ireland. I am the founder of EntySec, an open-source platform where I has developed multiple cybersecurity solutions, including the HatSploit Framework and the SeaShell Framework. I have actively participated in various Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions, achieving first place with team Loki at ZeroDays 2024 and winning the MIREA Cyberbeathlon twice with team ShadowLegacy. Additionally, I has showcased my work at prestigious cybersecurity conferences such as Positive Hack Days 2021 and OffZone 2022.
